+Donor_Blood_Bank 
	Donor_ID, Blood_Bank_ID		<- both FK of different tables

> A blood bank manager is assigned to each blood bank. A manager can only be assigned to one blood bank. (Unique)
> Hospitals always use blood from one assigned blood bank (Unique), and a blood bank can provide blood to several hospitals.
	Hospitals <-----one to one----> BloodBank (Unique)
	BloodBank -----one to many ----> Hospitals
> A donor can donate blood in several blood banks, and at least one donor donates blood to a blood bank (Means - if Donor has name in database then he/she must have to donate in a BloodBank).

	Donor -----one to many----> BloodBank


CREATE TABLE Donor_Blood_Bank (
    Donor_ID INT,
    Blood_Bank_ID INT,
    PRIMARY KEY (Donor_ID, Blood_Bank_ID),
    FOREIGN KEY (Donor_ID) REFERENCES Donor(Donor_ID),
    FOREIGN KEY (Blood_Bank_ID) REFERENCES Blood_Bank(Blood_Bank_ID)
);
